WHAT IS A SPACE SHUTTLE?
It is basically a rocket-launched spacecraft which is able to land like an unpowered aircraft, used to make repeated journeys between
the earth and space. It was comprised of four main components: the External Tank (ET); three Space Shuttle Main Engines; two Solid 
Rocket Boosters (SRBs); and the Orbiter vehicle.  These four components provided the shuttle with the ability to accomplish a diverse 
set of missions over its flight history.
